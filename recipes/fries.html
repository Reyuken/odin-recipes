Loaded Sriracha Fries

Photo by Allrecipes/Qi Ai

Description

Loaded Sriracha fries are a bold and flavorful twist on the classic French fry, combining the crispy texture of fries with a spicy, savory kick. To make them, freshly fried potato strips are piled high and topped with a mix of ingredients like melted cheese, green onions, and a generous drizzle of creamy Sriracha sauce. The heat of the Sriracha is perfectly balanced with a creamy, slightly tangy sauce, often made by mixing Sriracha with mayo or sour cream, creating an irresistible combination. The addition of ingredients like crispy bacon bits, jalape√±os, or cilantro further elevates the flavor profile, making them a decadent and mouthwatering snack or side dish.

Loaded Sriracha fries are often served as a crowd-pleasing appetizer or a fun and indulgent meal for those who enjoy a spicy, bold flavor. The crispy fries paired with the heat from the Sriracha sauce and the richness from the cheese and toppings make for a dynamic eating experience. Whether served at a casual get-together or enjoyed as a guilty pleasure, these fries are sure to satisfy cravings for something both spicy and savory.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds russet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/4-inch fries
  • 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons Sriracha sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons real bacon bits
  • 2 green onions, chopped

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place cut fries in a large bowl. Cover with cold water and let stand for 20 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) and line a large baking sheet with foil.
  4. Drain potatoes, rinse, and drain in a colander. Place potatoes back in the bowl.
  5. Whisk oil and Sriracha in a small bowl until smooth. Pour mixture over potatoes. Toss to coat.
  6. Arrange fries in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Flip fries and bake for 10 minutes more.
  7. Pile fries up into the center of the baking sheet with a spatula. Add shredded Cheddar and bacon bits. Return to the oven and bake until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
  8. Top with chopped green onions and serve immediately.
