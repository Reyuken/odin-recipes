Pickle Bacon Chopped Bagels

Photo by Peyton Beckwith

Description

Pickle Bacon Chopped Bagels are a bold and savory twist on the traditional bagel sandwich, combining the tangy crunch of pickles with the smoky richness of bacon. These bagels are typically chopped into bite-sized pieces and mixed with finely diced dill pickles, crispy bacon bits, and often a creamy, tangy spread like ranch or cream cheese to bring it all together. The result is a flavor-packed, texture-rich snack or meal that delivers a satisfying combination of salty, crunchy, and creamy in every bite.

Perfect for on-the-go breakfasts, brunch gatherings, or creative lunch options, Pickle Bacon Chopped Bagels have grown in popularity thanks to their viral appeal and crave-worthy taste. Whether you're a fan of pickles, a bacon enthusiast, or just looking to try something new, these bagels offer a unique and indulgent way to satisfy your savory cravings.

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces softened cream cheese
  • 1/3 cup dill pickle slices
  • 2 teaspoons pickle brine from the jar
  • 2 green onions, cut into 3-inch pieces
  • 4 slices cooked bacon
  • 4 dill sprigs
  • 2 everything bagels, sliced in half and toasted

Steps

  1. Place cream cheese onto a cutting board. Top cream cheese with pickles, brine, bacon, green onions, and dill. Chop all of the ingredients together and mix until well combined.
  2. Divide mixture onto the bottom halves of bagels; place top halves on. Cut in half and serve immediately.
